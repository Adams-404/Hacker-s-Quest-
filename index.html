About Hacker's Quest

Hacker's Quest is a text-based adventure game developed by Adams Cyber, where you play as a skilled hacker navigating through various challenges and missions. Use your wit and hacking skills to progress through the levels and uncover the secrets hidden within the digital realm.

×

Help

To play Hacker's Quest:

  1. Read the description of your current situation in the game text area.
  2. Choose a command from the options displayed in the command boxes, or type your own command.
  3. Enter your chosen command in the input field and click "Execute" or press Enter.
  4. Read the outcome of your action and continue making choices to progress through the game.

Your progress is automatically saved every 10 levels. Be careful, some choices may lead to game over!
